Kelly's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all time!

Moonrise Kingdom

Moonrise Kingdom is a whimiscal movie that focuses on two kids that run away together. It has beautiful cinematography and a quirky feel.

Pride and Prejudice

This movie adaption of my favorite book is a wonderful and captivating story no matter how many times I watch it.

Say Anything

This movie focuses on love and relationship. It tells the story of someone willing to risk it all for the girl he wants to be with.
